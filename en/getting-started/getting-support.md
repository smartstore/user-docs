# Getting Support

## Service Program

To ensure that your online shop is up and running in good time, we offer you qualified support. We take care of the initial installation for you or adjust the design according to your wishes - and all this with the competence of the manufacturer. For more information about this topic, please visit [our website](http://www.smartstore.com/net/en/#section-services).

## Support Program

Through a variety of support programs, we offer you the possibility to utilize the full potential of Smartstore. You have the choice to **book only the support you need**. For developers, agencies and end users alike, the Smartstore support programs offer the optimal solution for everyone. Additionally, you receive all exclusive premium plugins with the set-up of a support contract. For more information about this topic, please visit [our website](http://www.smartstore.com/net/en/#section-services).

## Community Support

If you don’t have a support contract with us, please feel free to use [our forum](http://community.smartstore.com/index.php?%2Fforum%2F85-smartstorenet-english%2F=&k=4bece6ba3e2c18927fe32d654cb5d9ba&setlanguage=1&cal_id=&langid=1) to get answers to your questions.
