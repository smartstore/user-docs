# Stories

## Story Templates

Stories can be saved as a template or exported. This allows existing stories to be used quickly and easily as templates for other stories. We already provide some templates for various use cases during the installation of the Page Builder.

You can also download more story templates from our [Marketplace](http://community.smartstore.com/index.php?/files/).
